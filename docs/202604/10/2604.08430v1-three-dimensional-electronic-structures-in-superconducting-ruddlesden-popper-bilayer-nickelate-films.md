## 摘要
超越铜氧化物的准二维（2D）范式，Ruddlesden-Popper 双层镍氧化物第三维度的作用对于解码其超导机制至关重要。在此，利用变光子能量角分辨光电子能谱（ARPES），我们系统地研究了通过低温超高真空手提箱转移的超导 (La,Pr,Sm)$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$/SrLaAlO$_4$ 薄膜（超导起始温度 $T_c^{\text{onset}} \sim 48$ K）的三维电子能带结构。我们揭示了轨道相关的维度特性：虽然以 $d_{x^2-y^2}$ 为主的能带表现出准二维特征，但以 $d_{z^2}$ 为主的能带显示出有限的 $k_z$ 色散。在跨越多个高对称方向的所有观测能带上都识别到了有限的能隙。系统的变温分析表征了 $d_{z^2}$ 衍生能带上能隙的超导性质，揭示了一个较大的能隙 $\Delta \sim 18$ meV，且其比值 $2\Delta/k_BT_c \sim 8$ 超过了弱耦合 BCS 极限。费米能级附近光谱权重的抑制在超导转变温度以上依然存在。普遍存在的类瀑布光谱特征证明了电子相互作用的存在。这些结果强调了 $d_{z^2}$ 轨道和关联效应的作用，为镍氧化物超导的理论模型提供了约束。

## Abstract
Beyond the quasi-two-dimensional (2D) paradigm of cuprates, the role of the third dimension of the Ruddlesden-Popper bilayer nickelates is essential to decoding their superconducting mechanism. Here, using angle-resolved photoemission spectroscopy (ARPES) with varied photon energies, we systematically investigate the electronic band structures in three dimensions for superconducting (La,Pr,Sm)$_3$Ni$2$O$7$/SrLaAlO$4$ thin films (superconducting onset temperature $T_c^{\text{onset}} \sim 48$ K) transferred via a cryogenic ultra-high vacuum suitcase. We reveal an orbital-dependent dimensionality: while the $d{x^2-y^2}$-dominant bands exhibit a quasi-2D character, the $d{z^2}$-dominant band displays a finite $k_z$ dispersion. Finite energy gaps are identified on all observed bands across multiple high-symmetry directions. Systematic temperature-dependent analysis characterizes the superconducting nature of the gap on the $d{z^2}$-derived band, revealing a large gap $Δ\sim 18$ meV and a ratio $2Δ/k_BT_c\sim 8$ exceeding the weak-coupling BCS limit. The suppression of spectral weight near the Fermi level persists above the superconducting transition temperature. Ubiquitous waterfall-like spectral features evidence the presence of electron interactions. These results underscore the role of the $d_{z^2}$ orbital and correlations, placing constraints on theoretical models for nickelate superconductivity.
