## 摘要
我们研究了非厄米磁性约瑟夫森结的输运性质，考虑了一个在外部磁场下与铁磁金属库耦合的超导体-量子点-超导体器件。我们重点关注了当磁场强度增加时，超导体之间的平衡相位差从 $\phi=0$ 变为 $\phi=\pi$ 时发生的 $0-\pi$ 相变。与环境的耦合诱导了自旋相关的耗散，并导致约瑟夫森结安德烈耶夫（Andreev）能级的展宽。通过将格林函数计算与局限于能隙下安德烈耶夫准束缚态的有效非厄米描述相结合，我们表明耗散将 $0-\pi$ 相变推向了更高的磁场。值得注意的是，在固定磁场强度下，外加磁场与储层磁化强度之间的相对夹角也可以用来驱动该相变。我们证明了这一效应可以完全归因于非厄米哈密顿量复本征值的行为。这些发现强调了非厄米性作为一种资源，可以为工程化超导结中的电流-相位关系引入新的调控手段。

## Abstract
We study the transport properties of non-Hermitian magnetic Josephson junctions, considering a superconductor-quantum dot-superconductor device coupled to a ferromagnetic metallic reservoir in the presence of an external magnetic field. We focus on the $0-π$ transitions that occur when the equilibrium phase difference between the superconductors shifts from $φ=0$ to $φ=π$ upon increasing the magnetic field amplitude. The coupling to the environment induces spin-dependent dissipation and leads to the broadening of the junction Andreev levels. By combining Green's function calculations with an effective non-Hermitian description restricted to the sub-gap Andreev quasi-bound states, we show that dissipation shifts the $0-π$ transition to higher magnetic fields. Remarkably, also the relative angle between the applied field and the reservoir magnetization can be used to drive the transition, at fixed field magnitude. We demonstrate that this effect can be entirely ascribed to the behavior of the complex eigenvalues of the non-Hermitian Hamiltonian. These findings highlight non-Hermiticity as a resource that can introduce new control knobs for engineering the current-phase relation in superconducting junctions.
