## 摘要
最近在菱面体石墨烯中的实验发现了“自掺杂”维格纳晶体（SDC）的证据，其中轻微非公度的维格纳晶体（WC）与一个小的费米海共存。我们提供了非微扰论证，表明此类 SDC 普遍产生于可公度晶体之间被抢占的能带反转转变，这为它们的出现提供了简单的能带理论判据。自洽哈特里-福克计算证实了在 $\lambda$-凝胶模型和菱面体五层石墨烯（R5G）中均存在与该机制一致的 SDC。在 $\lambda$-凝胶模型中，我们识别出一个位于“晕”-WC 和反常霍尔晶体（AHC）之间的 SDC 相，若钉扎在公度位置，这两者本应通过狄拉克转变相连；这与 WC-AHC 转变形成对比，我们证明由于对称性指标不匹配，后者无法通过连续转变相连。在 R5G 中，我们预测了一个位于 WC 和“被取消资格的”晕反常霍尔晶体之间的 SDC 相。我们一般性地讨论了母带中的贝里曲率分布如何影响 SDC 的出现，揭示了量子几何在诱导奇异量子物态中的新作用。

## Abstract
Recent experiments in rhombohedral graphene find evidence for a "self-doped" Wigner crystal (SDC) in which a slightly incommensurate Wigner crystal (WC) coexists with a small Fermi sea. We provide non-perturbative arguments that such SDCs generically arise from preempted band-inversion transitions between commensurate crystals, which motivates simple band-theory criteria for their appearance. Self-consistent Hartree-Fock calculations establish the existence of a SDC consistent with this mechanism in both the $λ$-jellium model and rhombohedral pentalayer graphene (R5G). In the $λ$-jellium model, we identify a SDC phase located between a "halo"-WC and an anomalous Hall crystal (AHC), which would otherwise be connected via a Dirac transition when pinned to commensuration; this contrasts with the WC-AHC transition, which we show cannot be connected by a continuous transition due to a mismatch of symmetry indices. In R5G, we predict a SDC phase located between a WC and a "disqualified" halo anomalous Hall crystal. We discuss in general how the Berry curvature distribution in the parent band affects the appearance of SDC, revealing a novel role of quantum geometry in inducing exotic quantum phases of matter.
