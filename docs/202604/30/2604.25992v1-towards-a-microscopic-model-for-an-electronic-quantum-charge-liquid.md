## 摘要
我们提供了一种构建电子量子电荷液体（QCL）的途径，这是一种由处于晶格分数填充且不破坏平移对称性的费米子构成的物态。从填充率为 $ν=3/2$ 的无自旋费米子出发，我们将它们配对，得到每个原胞填充率为 $ν=3/4$ 的玻色子。正方晶格上的四聚体模型（二聚体模型的推广）被评估为填充率为 $ν=3/4$ 的候选玻色型 QCL。研究表明，这些模型表现出局部 $\mathbb{Z}_4$ 对称性。通过对一系列四聚体波函数的数值研究发现，虽然其中一个由于 $\mathrm{U}(1)^3$ 对称性而无能隙，但至少有另一个可以被明确证明是有能隙的。该物态的有能隙性质及其 $\mathbb{Z}_4$ 对称性使我们提出，它是展示最小 $\mathbb{Z}_4$ 拓扑序的、难以捉摸的玻色型 QCL 的一个实例。最后，我们讨论了将其扩展到其他晶格几何结构、电子 QCL 以及里德堡原子的可能性。

## Abstract
We provide a route to constructing an electronic quantum charge liquid (QCL), a state made up of fermions at fractional filling of a lattice that does not break translation. Starting with spinless fermions at filling $ν=3/2$ we pair them to get bosons at filling $ν=3/4$ per unit cell. The tetramer model, a generalization of the dimer model, on the square lattice is evaluated as a candidate bosonic QCL at filling $ν= 3/4$. It is shown that these models exhibit a local $\mathbb{Z}_4$ symmetry. Upon numerical study of a family of tetramer wavefunctions it is found that while one is gapless due to $\mathrm{U}(1)^3$ symmetry at least one other can be definitively shown to be gapped. The gapped nature of this state, along with its $\mathbb{Z}_4$ symmetry, leads us to propose that it is an example of the elusive bosonic QCL displaying the minimal $\mathbb{Z}_4$ topological order. We conclude by discussing possible extensions to other lattice geometries, electronic QCLs, and to Rydberg atoms.
