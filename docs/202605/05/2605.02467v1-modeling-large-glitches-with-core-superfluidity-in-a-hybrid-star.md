## 摘要
许多脉冲星在其脉冲轮廓中表现出一种旋转周期突然增加的奇特行为，这通常被称为脉冲星自转突变（pulsar glitch）。其中一些表现出相对振幅为 ΔΩ/Ω ~ 10⁻⁶-10⁻⁵ 的巨自转突变。利用中子星（NS）地壳内钉扎中子涡旋的模型，目前还无法解释这种大自转突变。然而，越来越多的关于大质量脉冲星的证据表明，在脉冲星的内核中出现了奇异自由度。鉴于此，我们将脉冲星视为一颗混合星（HS）。该模型开启了核心内部涡旋钉扎的可能性。在吉布斯平衡条件下，强子和夸克相可能共存。由于全局电中性条件，在强子物质背景下形成了夸克面食（pasta）结构。我们将这些面食结构视为超流涡旋的钉扎点。我们表明，考虑到核心的贡献，我们的计算结果达到了 ΔΩ/Ω ~ 10⁻⁶ 的量级，这与类船帆座（Vela-like）脉冲星的观测结果接近。

## Abstract
Many pulsars exhibit a peculiar behaviour in their pulse profile of a sudden increase in their rotational period, which is popularly known as a pulsar glitch. Some of them show giant glitches with relative amplitude $ΔΩ/Ω\sim 10^{-6}-10^{-5}$. With the model of pinned neutron vortices inside the neutron star (NS) crust, this large glitch cannot be explained so far. However, the increasing evidence of massive pulsars indicates the appearance of exotic degrees of freedom in the inner core of the pulsars. Given this, we consider the pulsar as a hybrid star (HS). This model opens up the possibility of vortex-pinning inside the core. Under the Gibbs equilibrium conditions, it is possible for hadrons and the quark phase to coexist. Due to the global charge neutrality condition, quark pasta structures are formed in the background of hadronic matter. We consider these pasta structures as pinning sites of superfluid vortices. We show that considering the core contribution, our calculations come to be of the order of $ΔΩ/Ω\sim 10^{-6}$, which is close to the observations shown by the Vela-like pulsars.
